Research Project: Anthropogenic influences on nutrient dynamics and organic matter in Hewletts Creek, New Hanover County, North Carolina
Water quality in Hewletts Creek has been studied extensively for anthropogenic pollution, but earlier studies did not include analyses of colored dissolved organic matter (CDOM), dissolved organic carbon (DOC), or particulate organic matter (POM). Increases in CDOM, DOC, and POM can have significant negative impacts on water quality through depletion of oxygen and hampering the growth of photosynthetic organisms. Analysis was completed through water sampling, nutrient analysis, spectroscopy, and modeling using estuarine mixing curves.
